Mecha Legs
Passive


Each time his skill causes damage. Bruno's critical chance will be increased by 4%. Stacks up to 5 times.
Since Mecha Legs are infused with power, Bruno can deal more damage, but the extra ATK Speed only takes effect by 80%.

Volley Shot
Cooldown:10.0
Mana Cost:100 / 110 / 120 / 130 / 140 / 150

Bruno infuses the power to his Mecha Legs, his next Basic Attack will kick a powerball directly and hit an enemy target, dealing 110(+105% Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage and slowing the target by 60% for 0.5s.
The powerball can be rebounded to Bruno when hitting the target. Bruno of his teammatess can pick up the energy ball to reduce the CD of Flying Tackle by 1s and Bruno will have the powerball again.

Flying Tackle
Cooldown:7.0
Mana Cost:60 / 55 / 50 / 45 / 40 / 35

Bruno makes a forward slide tackle, stunning enemies for 0.5s and dealing 140 / 165 / 190 / 215 / 140 / 265 (+40% Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage.
If Bruno casts Flying Tackle while there is a powerball rebounding to him, he will pull back this powerball immediately.

Wave Of The World Animation
Cooldown:38.0 / 33.0 / 28.0
Mana Cost:140 / 160 / 180

Bruno kicks an energy ball with full power at a specified hero, dealing 250 / 350 / 450 (+95% Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage, knocking back the target and reducing enemy's Physical Defense by 3 / 6 / 9. Afterwards the energy ball will ricochet among enemy heroes (Up to 10 times). Every hit deals 150(+60% Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage. Physical Defense reducing effect lasts 8 seconds. Stacks up to 3 times.
