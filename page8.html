Lylia gets power from Gloom, increasing her Movement Speed by 15%. Each time Gloom levels up, she will increase extra Movement Speed by 5%.

Magic Shockwave
Cooldown:4.0
Mana Cost:50 / 55 / 60 / 65 / 70 / 75

Lylia releases an attack wave ahead, dealing 250(+100% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to enemies on the path and slowing them by 40%. Lasts 1.5s.
Gloom will hitch a ride on Magic Shockwave. If Gloom touches Shadow Energy, he will devour it and immediately detonate the energy.

Shadow Energy
Cooldown:9.0
Mana Cost:40 / 50 / 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100

Lylia condenses Shadow Energy to the designated area, dealing 100(+50% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to enemies nearby and slowing them by 80%.
After Gloom devours Shadow Energy, he will detonate the energy and deal 250(+100% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to enemies nearby. Each time Gloom upgrades himself, the damage dealt by Shadow Energy will be increased by 30%. Up to 120%. Lylia was born to have the ability to use Shadow Energy.

Lylia immediately returns to the position where the Black Shoe was 4s ago and stacks max times to use "Shadow Energy", regenerating 10% of Max HP and increasing her Movement Speed by 20%. Lasts 2s.
