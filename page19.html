Light Wheel Mark
Passive

Adds a Lightwheel Mark to the enemy at each basic attack or skill. When Lightwheel mark is added up to 5 marks at the target, they become actual lightwheel, piercing the target and dealing 7%-13% true damage of max HP, damage is added up to 300 towards jungle monsters.

Spinning Light Wheel
Cooldown:8
Mana Cost:80

Releases flywheels that stop at the target direction, dealing 200/235/270/305/340/375 physical damage on the path and slowing them by 80%, dealing 100/110/120/130/140/150 physical damage to the enemies nearby the target location and slowing them.

Phantom Step
Cooldown:6
Mana Cost:50

Move towards the target direction while releasing a flywheel to the most nearby enemy, dealing 150 damage. Releases 2 flywheels on ultimate state.

Dual Light Wheel
Cooldown:30
Mana Cost:140

Enters dual wield mode in the next 6/8/10s, increasing movement speed and releasing 2 lightwheels as basic attacks, but each basic attack only deals 50%/53%/56% damage and her attack speed is reduced by 20%.
