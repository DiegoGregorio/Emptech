Skills

Firaga Armor
     Passive

X.Borg is equipped with a homemade Firaga Armor that inherits his own Max HP, taking all damage for X.Borg while it's active. A short circuit will take place when the Armor runs out of HP, and X.Borg will do a roll to the direction of the Joystick to disengage from the armor. In this meantime, X.Borg won't take any damage. He then continues to restore energy in Armorless State. When energy is full. X.Borg will re-equip the armor and restore the armor to 30% of his Max HP.
X.Borg's fire damage causes enemies to warm up, and enemies with Highest Temperature will drop Firaga supplies when they take flame damage.

Fire Missiles
Cooldown:4.0 

X.Borg activates the flamethrower and fires in the specified direction, dealing 40 / 52 / 64 / 76 / 88 / 100 (+50%Total Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage for 2 seconds. Enemies whose Temperature rises to the limit will take True Damage to enemies and slow down by 30%.
Armorless State: X.Borg adjusts the flamethrower to narrow angle and lengthen distance, reducing the damage 60% of that in Armor State.

Fire Stake
Cooldown:11.0 / 9.5 / 9.0 / 8.5 / 8.0 / 7.5

X.Borg shoots 6 Fire Stakes at the edge of the fan-shaped indicator. 1.2s later, the stakes are taken back into his body. Each stake deals 50 / 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100 (+20%Total Physical Attack) Physical Damage to enemies in the path and pulls them back. If the Fire Stake touches Firaga supplies in its path, it will be take them back as well.
Armorless State: X.Borg adjusts the Fire Stake Launcher to send the stakes farther away and shortens the gap between the stakes.

Last Insanity
Cooldown:30.0 / 27.0 / 24.0 

X.Borg charges forward in a designated direction and starts the self-destruct program, spins around frantically and shoots fire around to deal 200 / 250 / 300 (+100% Extra Physical Attack) points of Physical Damage and slowing enemies by 30%. The whole process lasts 3s. Then he self-explodes to cause 400 / 550 / 700 and 15% of True Damage of target's Max HP. The explosion destroys the current Firaga Armor and puts himself under Armorless State, where this skill cannot be used. He can move again according to the direction of Joystick after he self-explodes.

Use again to end the flame spray and self-destruct right away.
