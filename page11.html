Skills

Dagger Specialist
Passive

Each spellcast adds a rune to Gusion's dagger. After stacking 3 runes, the next basic attack will deal additional damage equal to 15% of the target's missing HP, and restore HP equal to 85% of the damage deal.

Sword Spike
Cooldown:9.0
Mana Cost:70

Throws a dagger in the specified direction, dealing 200(+80% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to the first target hit. Use this skill again to appear behind the target and attack them for 200(+150% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage.

Shadowblade Slaughter
Cooldown:11.0
Mana Cost:65

Throws daggers forward, each dealing 130(+60% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to targets hit and slowing them for 2s. Use again to recall daggers, dealing 80(+50% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies hit as they return.

Incandescence
Cooldown:28.0
Mana Cost:100

Dashes to a designated location, immediately refresh Sword Spike and Shadowblade Slaughter cooldowns. This skill can be used again after a brief pause to dash a short distance in a specified direction. After being reset, Gusion will be able to immediately throw five daggers with Shadowblade Slaughter, and recover ten.
