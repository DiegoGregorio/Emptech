Deity Penalization
Passive

Rafaela penalizes the target who has killed her (the target should be within a certain range). She charges for 2 seconds and then sends the penalization power to the target, dealing True Damage equal to 25% of the target's Max HP, it can be blocked by other enemy heroes. This skill works only on enemy heroes.

Light Of Retribution
Cooldown:4.0
Mana Cost:70 / 85 / 100 / 115 / 130 / 145

Rafaela uses the power of Holy Light to deal 225 / 290 / 335 / 390 / 445 / 500 (+120% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to her three nearest enemies, reveals them for a short period of time, and reduces their movement speed by 60% for 1.5 seconds. If this skill hits the same target within 5 seconds, then the damage of this skill will increase by 10%. Stacks up to 5 times.
