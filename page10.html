Skills

Ashing
Passive

Valir's skills ignite his targets ablaze, every second dealing Magic Damage that equals to 0.6% of their Max HP, lasts for 4s. When blaze stacks 3 times on a target, it causes an explosion that deals Magic Damage to the target which equals to 8% of its Max HP, at the same time, stuns the target for 1s. (Within 7s, the same target can only be stunned Valir's skills once.)

Burst Fireball
Cooldown:1.5
Mana Cost:50 / 58 / 66 / 74 / 82 / 90

Valir casts a fireball at a designated direction and the fireball will explode upon hitting an enemy, dealing 280 / 310 / 340 / 370 / 400 / 430(+80% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies within the explosion range and slowing them by 40% for 1s. Meanwhile, it ignites the ground along the way, which leads to an explosion after several seconds, dealing 140 / 155 / 170 / 185 / 200 / 215(+40% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies within the area of effect.

Valir can stack the fireball up to 2 times. If the first target the fireball hits is an enemy hero, Valir will restore a fireball at once.

Searing Torrent
Cooldown:10.0
Mana Cost:65 / 70 / 75 / 80 / 85 / 90

Valir unleashes a torrent of flames forward, knocking enemies back and dealing 200 / 220 / 240 / 260 / 280 / 300(+60% Total Magic Power) points of Magic Damage to them.
After penetrating through Vengeance Flame, the torrent of flames will generate a firewall at its destination that lasts 4s, every second dealing 100 / 110 / 120 / 130 / 140 / 150(+30% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to nearby enemies.
The torrent of flames and firewall can slow enemies by 25% for 1s.

Vengeance Flame
Cooldown:30.0
Mana Cost:100 / 125 / 150

Valir removes all CC effects from him and condenses 4 Vengeance Flame around himself. His "Burst Fireball"/"Searing Torrent" will be enhanced by penetrating the Vengeance Flame and increase 30% damage for 9s. At the same time, increases his Movement Speed (decays with the time) by 60% in 5s.
